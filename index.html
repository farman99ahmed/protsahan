Our Story

Protsahan, a fest full of zeel and energy, is organized every year to give a chance to students to explore their extracurricular talent. Mukesh Patel School of Technology Management and Engineering, Shirpur celebrates this extravaganza every year in order to portray Trendy Skills of talented students. It aims to create an environment where people from diverse backgrounds can cross-pollinate ideas, share insights and gain new perspectives to create actionable outcomes to push the world forward. Last year, with our theme, The Indian Escapade, we presented a spectacular show along with various beguiling pre-events besides Nrityam, Melodia and Ramp-walk which gave us all the memories to cherish forever. The fest became a huge success when the famous Kushal Bharatia The Band and our one and only, cutipie Mohit Gaur energized the whole atmosphere with their enthralling performances. This year too, keep your bids high cause Protsahan 2020 is going to take your breaths away with the most captivating theme of all times The Comical Melange.
